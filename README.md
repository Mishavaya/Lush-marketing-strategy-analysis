# LUSH Marketing Strategy & Consumer Insights  

## 📊 Overview  
Developed a strategic analysis for Lush, combining market research, consumer journey mapping, and competitive analysis to evaluate brand positioning in the ethical beauty space.

---

## 🔍 Key Focus Areas  
- Industry & competitive landscape (clean beauty, sustainability trends)  
- Consumer behavior and decision-making journey  
- Brand positioning and community-driven marketing  
- SWOT analysis and growth opportunities  

---

## 💡 Strategic Recommendations  
- Build a **LUSH-owned ecosystem** to reduce reliance on social platforms  
- Scale the **“Friends of LUSH” creator network** for organic reach  
- Redesign the **loyalty program** to drive retention and advocacy  
- Expand into new segments (men’s grooming, wellness, gifting)  

---

## 📈 Impact  
Proposed strategies aim to increase:
- Website engagement and time-on-site  
- User-generated content and community growth  
- Loyalty program adoption and retention
